Here you'll find answers to common questions and ways to get in touch with our support team.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I add a transaction?

    Navigate to the "Dashboard" page, fill in the transaction form with the amount, description, and type, and click "Send".

  • How do I view my balance?

    Your current balance is displayed on the "Dashboard" page under the Balance section.

  • How can I reset my data?

    You can reset your data by clearing your browser's local storage or by clicking the "Reset Data" button in the settings.
